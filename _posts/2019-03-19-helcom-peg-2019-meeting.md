The HELCOM Phytoplankton Expert Group will meet in Daugavpils, Latvia, 8-12 April 2019. Iveta Jurgensone and Ieva Barda (Latvian Institute of Aquatic Ecology (LIAE)) are hosting the meeting this year. On the agenda this year, among other things, is a training course on diatoms, hosted by Diana Sarno (Department of Integrative Marine Ecology, Stazione Zoologica Anton Dohrn, Napoli, Italy). Check out the HELCOM-PEG gallery [here](http://nordicmicroalgae.org/galleries/helcom-peg)!
